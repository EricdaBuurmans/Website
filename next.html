NEXT

NEXT is a brand identity I created that hosts enviro-tech conferences and events. NEXT is all about focusing on the future and how we all can protect that future for the generations ahead of us. The events generally focus on the developments in technology for environmental benefit (e.g. climate change, pollution etc.). NEXT hosts hands on experiences for both tech enthusiasts and environmental advocates as well as informational talks from guest speakers. The goal of NEXT is to inform on how people can help be more sustainable and what Is being done to make things better, all in an engaging, modernised format.

This project had a range of design outputs including design briefs, logotype, identity systems, a brand manual, and a selection of collateral. The goal is to create a recognizable, scalable brand identity that positions NEXT as a hub for learning and action on environmental issues, all while considering sustainability, inclusivity, and cultural sensitivity.

NEXT aims to revitalise events and engage a youthful audience through interactive experiences. Unlike traditional counterparts, NEXT combines contemporary design with hands-on workshops to energize topics that often lack dynamism. The brand targets environmentally conscious tech enthusiasts, from young advocates to seasoned professionals, fostering motivation to learn and drive positive change.
