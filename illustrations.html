Illustrations

Below are some of my finished illustrations. I use the app Procreate to create the majority of my drawings. As you'll see, my inspirations lie in a mix of digital painting and graphic design. I also print these designs on t-shirts, posters, and other decor on my Redbubble shop.

Illustration is a hobby I use to unwind and I find it is a skill that proves useful and present in all of my other design work. Learning. Illustration has taught me different creative processes, as well as attention to detail and aesthetic awareness.
